Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria

Nigeria is the largest black nation in the world. It is a Western African with a population of over 200 million. It is rich in mineral resources with an abundance of hydrocarbons. Located on the Gulf of Guinea, it has many natural landmarks and wildlife reserves. Protected areas such as Cross River National Park and Yankari National Park have waterfalls, dense rainforest, savanna and rare primate habitats. One of the most recognizable sites is Zuma Rock, a 725m-tall monolith outside the capital of Abuja that is pictured on the national currency.
