About Us

My name is Udoeyen Okon, a registered Quantity Surveyor and certified Project Manager with over 15 years of experience in project management and contract administration. Backing up my experiences include a Project Management certificate, a bachelor’s degree in quantity surveying, and a master’s degree in construction management. With my team of professional Architects, Engineers, and surveyors, we established Kaevikon Ventures in 2012. Our goal is to optimize the commercial and quality successes of our client’s projects by delivering world-class services in Building and infrastructural development.

We have coordinated design and construction teams to initiate and execute over 300 complex maintenance and construction projects. We leverage our software skills in specialized software, including MS Project, Excel, Revit, AutoCAD, QS-Cad, and AI for project planning and scheduling to enhance project efficiency. We recently recovered over ₦8 billion in contractual claims for a client and successfully managed a 250-unit housing estate project, completing it on time and within budget. We are detail-oriented, ethical, transparent, and committed to contributing to your projects to ensure 100% performance and client satisfaction.

We Offer:

• Project design and construction, including feasibility studies, surveying, and site plan analysis. • Contract of Construction. • Core Quantity Surveying services like preparation of Bill of Quantities (BOQ), work valuations, claims, sub-contractors’ accounts, and final account settlement. • Project management to deliver projects within the important constraints of scope, cost, schedule, and quality. • Contract Administration through pre-construction, construction, and post-contract stages. • Dispute resolutions utilizing the Arbitration and Mediation system. • Equipment lease. • Intenship for tradesmem and student professionals
